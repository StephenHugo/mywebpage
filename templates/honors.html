Honors and Awards

2010 - 2014
McKnight Doctoral Fellowship Recipient
- Without the dedicated work or Dr. Lawrence Morehouse and Mr. Charles Jackson through the Florida Education Fund, I would not have been able to afford graduate school. It was an honor to be a part of the program, and I hope I can return a fraction of the support I received in the past 4 years.

2013
OGMP Supplemental Retention Award
- The Office of Graduate Minority Programs has also done an outstanding job supporting and guiding me through my Masters and PhD experience. This award allowed me to get by while finishing my dissertation, when I typically had to work as a grader or TA to help support myself each year. Dr. David W. Mazyck did a phenomenal job as the interim Dean and helped me find the support I needed at a critical time.

2013
South East Alliance for Graduate Education and the Professoriate Award
- Dr. Anne Donnelly manages a lot of things at UF, and SEAGEP is one of them. Mr. Earl Wade from the OGMP pointed me in her direction and she took care of the rest to help me earn this award.

2011
President of the Graduate Association of Chemical Engineers, University of Florida
- Having been a part of the ChemE program since my Masters, I took the reigns of our graduate student association as I entered PhD candidacy. In that year, we began several new ideas to improve GRACE, including more social events and a new facebook page to enhance our network.
