Welcome!

My name is Stephen. I was recently picked up by the Biomedical Engineering department at the University of Florida as a lecture professor!

My responsibilities include managing the department teaching lab, including the capstone senior design course, bioinstrumentation and intro to BME labs. Our department sources project ideas from the real-world clinical needs of clinical partners from all over UF, which are identified through a shadowing program available to our graduate students. Seniors then refine those ideas, brainstorm new devices to meet clinical needs, and develop prototypes by the end of the academic year.

I'm working to make stronger ties with UF's Office of Technology and Licensing and introduce my students to entrepreneurship early, and also strengthen our connections with local industry to create internship/job opportunities. A good proportion of our students do plan to stay in academia or go on to medical school as well, so it will be a diverse crowd with lots of creative potential!
