Welcome!

My name is Stephen. I received my PhD in chemical engineering from the University of Florida in August, 2014. My thesis initially focused on single-cell mechanics and migration, a field known as biophysics. As I learned more about cell behaviour and gathered data, image processing became a necessary component to my project. The experience granted me a unique perspective and multidisciplinary skill set, which I want to utilize in the future to teach and directly contribute to new technologies.

This website was created with those goals in mind.
