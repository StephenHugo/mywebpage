Welcome!

My name is Stephen. I received my PhD in chemical engineering from the University of Florida in August, 2014. My thesis initially focused on single-cell mechanics and migration, a field known as biophysics. As I learned more about cell behaviour and gathered data, image processing became a necessary component to my project. In the future, I hope to utilize my unique biological perspective and skill set to improve our current understanding of medical imaging through education and direct contributions.

This page was created with those goals in mind. For now, I've added a program to the Projects section that accepts a link to an image and returns a processed version of that image. Please go take a look!
