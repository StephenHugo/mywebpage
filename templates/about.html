About Me

Please, check out my Github to get the code for this page and other projects I can share. I am fluent in MATLAB and now python, with moderate experience with C++, java, javascript, and good knowledge of ImageJ. On the other hand, I'm experienced with cell culture, protein purification, molecular biology, rheology and microscopy. Before joining the Chemical Engineering department at UF, I had volunteer experience at Arnold Palmer children's Hospital in Orlando, Fl, as well as an internship at the Jacksonville Cardiovascular Center in Jacksonville, FL. Before that, I had received a first responder's licence from Santa Fe College in Gainesville, Fl. I have a passion for people and health, and the capability to improve their lives through education and hard work.

Feel free to contact me.
